Here’s a structured presentation outline exclusively for the **YMAL Application**:

---

### **Slide 1: Title Slide**
- **Title**: **YMAL (You May Also Like) Application**
- **Subtitle**: Enhancing Personalized Recommendations for Starbucks Customers  

---

### **Slide 2: Introduction**
**Definition**:  
YMAL (You May Also Like) is a Starbucks application designed to provide personalized alternative product recommendations.  

**Key Objective**:  
To act as a challenger to the existing GetRecs system by offering alternative suggestions tailored to individual customer preferences.  

---

### **Slide 3: Key Features**
1. **Alternative Recommendations**:  
   - Offers alternative suggestions based on customer transaction data and preferences.  
2. **Integration with GetRecs**:  
   - Works alongside GetRecs to enhance the diversity of recommendations.  
3. **Continuous Personalization**:  
   - Continuously refines recommendations using real-time customer data.  

---

### **Slide 4: Workflow**
1. **Data Collection**:  
   - Gathers customer transaction data, preferences, and contextual information.  
2. **Feature Extraction**:  
   - Processes data for product categories, customer patterns, and seasonal trends.  
3. **Model Training**:  
   - Builds a machine learning model to generate personalized suggestions.  
4. **Recommendation Generation**:  
   - Creates recommendations displayed under the 'You May Also Like' section.  
5. **Integration with GetRecs**:  
   - Compares and complements GetRecs recommendations for a better user experience.  

---

### **Slide 5: Architecture**
- **Technology Stack**:  
   - **PySpark**: Data processing and feature extraction.  
   - **Azure**: Hosting and managing cloud resources.  
   - **Airflow**: Orchestration and workflow scheduling.  
   - **Machine Learning Models**: Powering recommendation generation.  

- **Integration with Starbucks App**:  
   - Recommendations are displayed in real-time under the 'You May Also Like' section for seamless user interaction.  

---

### **Slide 6: Challenges and Solutions**
1. **Data Quality**:  
   - **Challenge**: Noisy or incomplete data.  
   - **Solution**: Implemented data validation and cleansing.  

2. **Integration with GetRecs**:  
   - **Challenge**: Synchronizing recommendations with GetRecs.  
   - **Solution**: Conducted rigorous testing to ensure seamless integration.  

---

### **Slide 7: Testing and Evaluation**
1. **Performance Monitoring**:  
   - Regularly compares YMAL’s recommendations with GetRecs.  
2. **Key Metrics**:  
   - Customer engagement rates.  
   - Relevance and accuracy of recommendations.  
   - Conversion rates.  

---

### **Slide 8: Conclusion**
YMAL represents Starbucks’ innovative approach to personalized recommendations. By leveraging machine learning and customer data, YMAL provides alternative product suggestions that enhance the customer experience and foster loyalty, complementing the existing GetRecs system.
