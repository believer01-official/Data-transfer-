### **Slide 4: Technology Stack**  
**Content**:  
- **Azure Data Factory (ADF)**: For orchestrating data pipelines.

- **Cosine Similarity**: For calculating product similarities.  
- **Redis**: For fast data retrieval.

### **Slide 5: Usuals Aggregate Pipeline**  
**Content**:  
- **Goal**: Provide the user with a "Quick Order" card for specific times of the day and week.  
- **Details**:  
  - Aggregates both in-store and mobile app transactions.  
  - Identifies customer preference patterns.  
- **Technology**: ADF for data orchestration.  
- **Schedule**: Weekly on Saturdays.  
- **Runtime**: 7 hours.  

---

### **Slide 6: Seasonal Product Boosting Pipeline**  
**Content**:  
- **Goal**: Optimize recommendations for seasonal products.  
- **Details**:  
  - Analyzes transaction data to identify seasonal product preferences.  
  - Boosts seasonal product selection likelihood in recommendations.  
- **Technology**: ADF for data processing.  
- **Schedule**: Weekly on Saturdays.  
- **Runtime**: 33 minutes.  

---

### **Slide 7: KPI Pipeline**  
**Content**:  
- **Goal**: Process clickstream data from the Starbucks mobile application.  
- **Details**:  
  - Tracks user interactions and behaviors.  
  - Writes intermediary data to ADLS Delta Tables and SQL Tables.  
- **Technology**: ADF for orchestration.  
- **Schedule**: Daily.  
- **Runtime**: 15-17 minutes.  

---

### **Slide 8: Load and Switch Views Pipeline**  
**Content**:  
- **Goal**: Sync data between Usuals Delta Paths and SQL DB for quick access.  
- **Details**:  
  - Reads data from Delta Tables and loads it into SQL.  
  - Maintains database views for optimal performance.  
- **Technology**: ADF for execution.  
- **Schedule**: Weekly on Tuesday and Wednesday.  
- **Runtime**: Tuesday - 20 minutes, Wednesday - 4 hours.  

---

### **Slide 9: Similar_items_Master Pipeline**  
**Content**:  
- **Goal**: Generate category similarity data for the "Similar Items" feature.  
- **Details**:  
  - Uses cosine similarity to compute relationships between menu items.  
  - Stores results in ADLS Delta Paths.  
- **Technology**: ADF for orchestration.  
- **Schedule**: Weekly on Saturdays.  
- **Runtime**: 25 minutes.  

---

### **Slide 10: Upload_to_redis Pipeline**  
**Content**:  
- **Goal**: Upload similarity data from Delta Paths to Redis for fast retrieval.  
- **Details**:  
  - Ensures low-latency data availability for applications.  
- **Technology**: ADF for execution.  
- **Schedule**: Weekly on Saturdays.  
- **Runtime**: 10 minutes.
